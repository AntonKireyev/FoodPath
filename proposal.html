WebTech Project Proposal

Objective of the Website

The objective of the website is to provide a comprehensive review of restaurants that can be found in and around the Boston area.

Target Audience

The target audience would be college students looking for some tasty as well as budget friendly options around their campus.

Content Plan for Each Page

In addition to the homepage, sitemap, and content/feedback page there will be 4 additional pages of content.

  1. Restaurant Ranking

    This page will include the top 3 choices of the month that I have personally tried in the past.

  2. Restaurant Map

    Users will be able to see restaurants that are near them.

  3. "Best" Places to eat

    This page will forego the "budget" considerations and provide lists of restaurants that are the best in the category of food.

  4. Food Trends Page

    This could include data and information regarding market share of certain food types or rising popularity of new restaurants in the area.
